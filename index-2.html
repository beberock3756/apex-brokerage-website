Business Valuation

How we evaluate your business

Every engagement starts with a clear-eyed look at what your business is actually worth — before any conversation about a sale.

01

Discovery call

A free, no-obligation conversation about your business, your goals, and your timeline — whether that's six months or six years out.

02

Financial review

We review revenue, margins, add-backs, and cash flow (SDE/EBITDA) to understand the true earning power of the business.

03

Market comparables

We benchmark against recent Tampa Bay and Florida transactions in your industry to ground the number in real market data.

04

Valuation report

You receive a clear, written estimate of value with the reasoning behind it — plain language, no jargon.

05

Strategy session

We walk through what could increase value before a sale, and lay out a realistic timeline if you decide to move forward.
